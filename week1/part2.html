Methodology and Development Processes - Presentation

    The roles within Howlers product team.

  • Product manager: The product manager is like the coach in a soccer team. The product manager determines what direction the team will be heading and also what work will be done. (Chris) Also the product manager manages external relationships that work hand in hand with the product. The product manager also manages current blockers that are standing in the way of productivity

  • Product designer: Product designers use their design skills and technical knowledge to improve the way that existing products work and look, and/or produce them at a lower cost.(Okuhle)

  • Developers: Software developer. Becoming a software developer, also known as a computer programmer, you'll be playing a key role in the design, installation, testing and maintenance of software systems.

  • Quality Assurance: A quality assurance ensures that the final product observes the company's quality standards. In general, these detail-oriented professionals are responsible for the development and implementation of inspection activities, the detection and resolution of problems, and the delivery of satisfactory outcomes.(Thabi)

  • Product success manger: A Customer Success Manager is responsible for developing customer relationships that promote retention and loyalty. Their job is to work closely with customers to ensure they are satisfied with the services they receive and to improve upon areas of dissatisfaction. (Lourens & Hugo)
